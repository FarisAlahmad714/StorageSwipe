‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I organize my photos and videos?
StorageSwipe helps you categorize and manage your media files efficiently. Use the app's intuitive interface to sort, tag, and organize your content.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All your photos and videos are stored locally on your device. We prioritize your privacy and do not upload your personal media to external servers.
How do I delete unwanted media?
You can easily delete photos and videos directly through the app. Use the selection tools to choose multiple items for batch deletion.
Can I recover deleted items?
Deleted items may be recoverable from your device's Recently Deleted folder, depending on your device's settings. We recommend being cautious when deleting media.
The app is crashing. What should I do?
Try restarting the app first. If the problem persists, please contact us via email with details about your device model and when the crash occurs.
